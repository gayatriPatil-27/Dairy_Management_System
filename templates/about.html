About Us

Know About Our Dairy Farm & Our History

The company has its own state of art at Jaysingpur, Maharashtra. All in all the Company has 2 Chilling centre and one main Dairy located at Jaysingpur, . The company has a wide distribution network across western Maharashtra. The products and services are offered through various Sales and Marketing offices across the region. The company offers wide range of Milk Products Comprising of fresh, refreshing and nutritious Milk, Butter, . These products are conveniently packed to suit various needs of consumers. The company has been rapidly growing during the 8 years since its inception. Today it is one of the popular dairy companies in Maharashtra. The consistent growth of the organization reflects the customer confidence on the products and services offered.

Dedicated Services
Provide quality milk and milk products to our consumers at reasonable prices while ensuring fair returns to the producers. Be a company with a diversified portfolio of consumer food brands and products that are market leaders in their respective categories. Simultaneously to be one of the most preferred organizations to work for and deal with in the country
Organic Products
The fresh milk from the Buffalo and cows is procured by the farmers Milk Societies and brought to the nearest chilling centers and then to the processing Plants with in a stipulated time period without breaching the cold chain. This milk is the pasteurized at the processing factory. After all the stringent processing the milk is packed in high quality food grade and user friendly pouches.
